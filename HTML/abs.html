About Us

Arogini Blood Bank System is a noble charity organization born from the active desire to donate. An organization created by a group of young social workers, aiming to deal with the problems of modern society. In order to prepare social citizens that are tolerant, responsive, accountable, and responsible, we aim to enhance the humanistic aspects of each individual. Arogini Blood Bank strives to raise blood donation awareness among the public. This organization is committed to remain ahead of all linguistic-rational-religious-political differences and shall focus its medical activities on technology support with a full focus on health care activities.
